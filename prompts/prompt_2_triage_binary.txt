Prompt 2: Screening Assistant / Triage — Binary Format

Purpose:
This prompt asks the model to act as an abstract screening assistant. The goal is to identify studies that may be relevant enough for human full-text review.

Important note:
This is not a final eligibility decision. Human reviewers will validate the final inclusion decision.

Study Details:
{study_details}

Screening Task:
You are assisting with title and abstract screening for a systematic review of youth homelessness interventions.

Your goal is to identify studies that are potentially relevant enough to move forward for human review.

This is an initial triage stage. Do not require the abstract to prove final eligibility.

Pre-Screening Focus:

1. Original or Empirical Research
Include studies that appear to use primary data, direct observations, evaluation data, implementation data, qualitative data, quantitative data, or mixed methods.
Exclude editorials, letters, protocols, systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and purely conceptual papers.

2. Youth or Young People
Include if the study refers to youth, adolescents, young adults, young people, runaway youth, street youth, students, minors, transition-age youth, or mixed-age samples that may include youth ages 13–25.
Exclude only if the study clearly focuses solely on people outside the youth age range.

3. Homelessness, Housing Instability, or Risk
Include if the population is homeless, unstably housed, runaway, street-involved, shelter-involved, couch surfing, doubled up, leaving foster care, involved in homelessness services, or at risk of homelessness.
Include if the study setting or system is clearly connected to youth homelessness or housing instability.
Exclude only if there is clearly no connection to homelessness, housing instability, runaway status, street involvement, or homelessness risk.

4. Program, Service, Intervention, or Systems Focus
Include if the study examines a program, service, intervention, policy, practice, outreach model, case management model, housing program, health service, school-based service, family intervention, behavioral health intervention, systems-level response, or other support relevant to homeless or at-risk youth.
Exclude only if there is no program, service, intervention, policy, or systems focus.

Decision Instructions:
Recommend Include if the study clearly or plausibly matches the pre-screening focus areas.
Recommend Exclude only if the study is clearly incompatible with the review.
When abstracts are ambiguous or incomplete, lean toward Include to reduce missed studies.

Do not exclude based only on missing effectiveness results, implementation details, or full geographic confirmation. These can be assessed during full-text review.

Return a list of exclusion codes for criteria not met:

X-1: Not original or empirical research
X-2: Target population clearly not youth or young people
X-3: No connection to homelessness, housing instability, runaway status, street involvement, or homelessness risk
X-4: No program, service, intervention, policy, or systems focus

Return only a JSON object in the following format:

{
  "Decision": "Include" or "Exclude",
  "Explanation_Codes": ["X-code"] or [],
  "Focus_Area_Results": {
    "Original_Empirical": "Yes" or "No",
    "Youth_Age_13_25": "Yes" or "No",
    "Homelessness_Target": "Yes" or "No",
    "Program_Focus": "Yes" or "No"
  }
}

Return only the JSON object. No extra text.
