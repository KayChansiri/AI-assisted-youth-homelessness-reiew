Prompt 1: Strict Eligibility Screening — Binary Format

Purpose:
This prompt asks the model to behave like a strict systematic review screener. It evaluates multiple eligibility criteria and includes a study only when all criteria appear to be satisfied.

Important note:
This is a strict title/abstract screening prompt. It may be too conservative when abstracts do not provide enough detail.

Study Details:
{study_details}

Screening Task:
You are an expert systematic review screener. Your task is to determine whether the study should be included in a systematic review of interventions for youth experiencing homelessness or housing instability.

Evaluate each criterion independently.

Eligibility Criteria:

1. Original or Empirical Research
Include only if the study appears to be based on original empirical research, primary data, intervention evaluation, implementation data, or direct observations.
Exclude editorials, commentaries, letters, protocols, systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and studies based only on secondary analysis unless clearly tied to intervention evaluation.

2. Youth Population
Include only if the study focuses on youth, adolescents, young adults, or young people within or overlapping ages 13–25.
Exclude if the study clearly focuses only on children younger than 13 or adults older than 25.

3. Homelessness or Housing Instability
Include only if the population is homeless, unstably housed, runaway, street-involved, shelter-involved, couch surfing, doubled up, leaving foster care and at risk of homelessness, or otherwise clearly connected to homelessness or housing instability.
Exclude if there is no apparent connection to homelessness, housing instability, runaway status, street involvement, or homelessness risk.

4. Intervention, Program, Service, or Policy Focus
Include only if the study evaluates, describes, or examines an intervention, program, service model, policy, practice, or systems-level response.
Exclude if the study only describes prevalence, risk factors, needs, or background conditions without any program, service, intervention, or policy focus.

5. Relevant Outcomes or Implementation Information
Include only if the study appears to report outcomes, effectiveness, implementation, service delivery, access, engagement, retention, participation, or other intervention-related evidence.
Exclude if no outcome, implementation, or intervention-related information is apparent.

6. Relevant Country Context
Include only if the study appears to take place in an OECD or comparable high-income country context.
Exclude if the country context is clearly outside the intended review scope.

7. Relevance to Youth Homelessness Intervention Evidence
Include only if the study clearly contributes to evidence about interventions, services, systems, or programs for youth experiencing homelessness, housing instability, or clear risk of homelessness.

8. Sufficient Abstract Information
Include only if the title, abstract, and metadata provide enough information to judge that the study meets all criteria.

Decision Rule:
Recommend Include only if all eligibility criteria are met or strongly implied.
Recommend Exclude if one or more eligibility criteria are not met or cannot be determined from the abstract and metadata.

Return a list of exclusion codes for criteria not met:

X-1: Not original or empirical research
X-2: Target population not youth ages 13–25
X-3: Population not homeless, unstably housed, runaway, street-involved, or at risk of homelessness
X-4: No program, service, intervention, policy, or systems focus
X-5: No relevant outcome, effectiveness, or implementation information
X-6: Country context outside review scope
X-7: Not relevant to youth homelessness intervention evidence
X-8: Insufficient information to determine eligibility

Return only a JSON object in the following format:

{
  "Decision": "Include" or "Exclude",
  "Explanation_Codes": ["X-code"] or [],
  "Eligibility_Results": {
    "Original_Empirical": "Yes" or "No",
    "Youth_Age_13_25": "Yes" or "No",
    "Homelessness_Target": "Yes" or "No",
    "Program_Focus": "Yes" or "No",
    "Outcome_or_Implementation": "Yes" or "No",
    "Country_Context": "Yes" or "No",
    "Youth_Homelessness_Relevance": "Yes" or "No",
    "Sufficient_Information": "Yes" or "No"
  }
}

Return only the JSON object. No extra text.
